About StocPre

StocPre is a stock trading aid which aims to help new traders by providing them with predictions about the future prices of stocks.

How?

StocPre predicts the prices of stocks using machine learning. By training LSTM models on historical data, the machine learns the patterns of the stocks movements. Thus, it is able to make informed predictions about the future prices.

Getting Started

To access all of the features in StocPre simply sign up and log in. Once you've logged in you'll be able to see the stocks readily available on the site and more.
